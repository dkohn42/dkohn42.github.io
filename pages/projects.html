Robby the Robot - Q-Learning

Q-Learning is a type a reinforcement learning called Q-Learning. States the robot could precieve are mapped to an action in a Q-table. The condition of 'picking up a can when standing on a can' is given a reward value. Over millions of iterations, these rewards are back-propogated through the table to provide the robot with a learned strategy for every possible state. arning 'robot' to navigate an area and pickup as many cans as possible. The robot can only precieve what it is standing on and what is immediately to its up, down, left or right direction.
